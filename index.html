Treasures of the Forest

Woodman Company Inc. is proud to source its timber from the finest and most sustainable local forests. The forests around Forks, Washington, are abundant and beautiful, filled with life-giving trees from which we obtain premium quality raw materials. Each piece of timber is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality and durability.

Our commitment to sustainable forestry practices means that we work closely with local environmental organizations to maintain the health and vitality of our forests. By selectively harvesting trees and promoting new growth, we ensure that future generations will continue to benefit from these natural resources. Our timber is not only high-quality but also eco-friendly, reflecting our dedication to environmental stewardship.

Our Workshop and Timber Yards

At Woodman Company Inc., our workshops and timber yards are equipped with the latest technology and staffed by top-notch experts. Every piece of wood is meticulously processed to ensure only the finest quality reaches your home. Our master carpenters and joiners breathe life into the wood, transforming it into stunning forms. Throughout our processes, we prioritize sustainability and environmental care.

Our team of skilled artisans brings years of experience and a passion for woodworking to every project. From the initial selection of the timber to the final finishing touches, we ensure that each piece meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship. Our state-of-the-art facilities allow us to produce a wide range of products, from custom furniture to architectural millwork, all designed to enhance the beauty and functionality of your home.
